生化是音乐区

有关生化的音乐创作不仅多还洗脑。。。

Hemoglobin’s moving around是Oregon State University的生化教授Kevin Ahern根据”Santa Claus is Coming to Town”的旋律填词创作的。

旋律很好听，快点开跟我一起被洗脑欣赏~

原歌词及翻译：

A metabolic melody by Kevin Ahern (To the tune of “Santa Claus is Coming to Town”) Vocal by Tim Karplus

Oh isn’t it great

蛋白质真厉害

What proteins can do

能做很多事

Especially ones that bind to O2

特别是那些运输氧气的

Hemoglobin’s moving around

血红蛋白转啊转

Inside of the lungs

在我们的肺里

It picks up the bait

它载上某些分子

And changes itself from T to R state

从T态变成R态

Hemoglobin’s moving around

血红蛋白转啊转

The proto-porphyrin system

原卟啉环上

Its iron makes such a scene

铁离子在刷存在感

Arising when an O2 binds

结合氧气时挪一下

Pulling up on histidine

还拉上组氨酸

The binding occurs cooperatively

结合是协同的

Thanks to changes qua-ter-nar-y

因为四级结构的改变

Hemoglobin’s moving around

血红蛋白转啊转

It exits the lungs

它满载氧气

Engorged with O2

从肺中离开

In search of a working body tissue

要找在代谢的身体组织

Hemoglobin’s moving around

血红蛋白转啊转

The proton concentration

氢离子浓度

Is high and has a role

也有影响

Between the alpha betas

在α和β链之间

It finds imidazole

氢离子找到了咪唑环

To empty their loads

为了清空负载

The globins decree

它们决定

“We need to bind 2,3BPG”

“我们要带上2,3BPG”

Hemoglobin’s moving around

血红蛋白转啊转

The stage is thus set

就这样万事俱备

For grabbing a few

Cellular dumps of CO2

为了抓住细胞丢弃的二氧化碳

Hemoglobin’s moving around

血红蛋白转啊转

And then inside the lungs it discovers ox-y-gen

然后在肺里它又发现了氧气

And dumps the CO2 off

就丢下二氧化碳

To start all o’er again

重新再来

So see how this works

要知道这是怎么运作的

You better expect

最好是

To have to describe the Bohr effect

用玻尔效应描述

Hemoglobin’s moving around

血红蛋白转啊转
